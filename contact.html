Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about contacting us and customer support

What is the typical response time for inquiries?

We typically respond to email inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, we recommend using our live chat feature in the app or calling our customer support number for immediate assistance.

How can healthcare providers join the platform?

Healthcare providers interested in joining our platform can fill out the registration form in the "For Doctors" section of our website or contact our provider relations team at providers@specialistdoctors.com. We'll guide you through the verification process and platform onboarding.

Do you offer technical support outside of business hours?

Yes, our technical support team is available 24/7 through the in-app chat feature. For critical technical issues affecting healthcare delivery, we also maintain an emergency support line that can be accessed through the app.

How can I provide feedback about the app?

We welcome feedback about your experience using Specialist Doctors! You can provide feedback through the contact form on this page by selecting "Feedback" in the Inquiry Type dropdown, or directly within the app under Settings > Help & Support > Provide Feedback.

I'm having trouble with my account. Who do I contact?

For account-related issues, please contact our account support team at accounts@specialistdoctors.com or call our support line at +1 (800) 123-4567. Be sure to have your account information ready to help us assist you more efficiently.
