Common Questions from Providers

Find answers to frequently asked questions about joining our platform

What specialties can join the platform?

We welcome healthcare providers from all medical specialties. Currently, our platform supports over 30 specialties including cardiology, dermatology, endocrinology, gastroenterology, neurology, oncology, pediatrics, psychiatry, and many more. If your specialty isn't listed during registration, please contact us to discuss how we can accommodate your practice.

How long does the verification process take?

Our verification process typically takes 2-3 business days once all required documentation is submitted. We verify your medical license, credentials, education, and professional standing to ensure the highest quality of care on our platform. For expedited verification, you can select our priority verification option during registration.

How does payment and billing work?

Our platform handles all payment processing for your virtual consultations. Patients pay through the app, and we deposit earnings directly to your account bi-weekly. We support various payment methods including insurance billing, with automated verification of patient coverage. You can set your own consultation fees or use our recommended pricing structure based on your specialty and region.

What equipment do I need to get started?

You'll need a reliable computer or tablet with a high-quality camera and microphone, plus a stable internet connection (minimum 5 Mbps upload/download speed recommended). For optimal patient experiences, we suggest using a headset with noise-cancellation and ensuring good lighting in your consultation space. Some specialties may benefit from additional equipment like digital stethoscopes or examination cameras, which can integrate with our platform.

Can I integrate with my existing EHR system?

Yes, our platform supports integration with most major EHR systems including Epic, Cerner, Allscripts, and many others. Our technical team will work with you during onboarding to set up a secure data exchange between systems. For practices using less common EHR solutions, we offer an API-based approach for custom integration or can provide guidance on manual data synchronization processes.
