Common Questions from Patients

Find answers to your questions about using our platform

How quickly can I get an appointment?

Most patients can get same-day or next-day appointments with general practitioners and within a week for specialists. For urgent issues, we have on-demand consultation options available with doctors who are currently online. The exact wait time depends on the specialty and provider availability.

Can I use my insurance to cover consultations?

Yes, we accept most major insurance plans, Medicare, and Medicaid. When you book an appointment, you can enter your insurance details, and our system will verify your coverage and provide an estimate of your out-of-pocket costs. For those without insurance, we offer transparent self-pay rates.

What conditions can be treated through telehealth?

A wide range of conditions can be effectively diagnosed and treated through telehealth, including chronic disease management, mental health conditions, dermatological issues, and follow-up care. Some conditions may require physical examination or tests that can't be done remotely, in which case your doctor will let you know if an in-person visit is necessary.

How do I prepare for a video consultation?

For the best experience, find a quiet, well-lit private space with a stable internet connection. Have your health history, current symptoms, and medication list ready to share. If relevant, take photos of visible symptoms before your appointment to share with the doctor. Test your camera and microphone before the call. You'll receive detailed instructions and a link to join the video consultation in your confirmation email.

Can doctors prescribe medication through the app?

Yes, doctors can prescribe many medications through the app, including medications for chronic conditions, infections, and other non-controlled substances. Prescriptions are sent electronically to your preferred pharmacy. Some controlled substances may not be eligible for telehealth prescription due to federal regulations. Your doctor will explain your options during the consultation.
